about us

Fountain Of Glory is the largest tissue reel manufacturer in West Africa. It employs more than two hundred personnel to produce quality and cost effective products. It has three paper machines with an annual capacity of more than forty-five thousand metric ton (45,000 MT) of jumbo reels. it is capable of processing pure pulp and recycled paper. its products include bathroom tissue, kitchen towel, napkin and facial. its grades vary according to customer requirements from economy, special and premium products. it supplies its affiliate Fountain impex, a market leader of quality tissue products and producer of the highly popular Rose carla. Also, it is the major tissue reel provider for local converters and similarly exports to the African region.
